One-time Firefox setup required for auto-fullscreen
1 Open Firefox settings
Type about:config in your address bar and press Enter. Click "Accept the Risk and Continue" if prompted.
2 Search for the setting
In the search bar, paste:
full-screen-api.allow-trusted-requests-only
3 Toggle it to false
Click the toggle button on the right side to change the value from true to false.
4 Done!
Close the about:config tab. Saskatoon can now automatically restore fullscreen during episode transitions.
